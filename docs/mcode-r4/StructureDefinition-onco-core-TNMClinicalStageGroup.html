TNMClinicalStageGroup

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/mcode/StructureDefinition/onco-core-TNMClinicalStageGroup

The extent of the cancer in the body, according to the TNM classification system, based on information obtained prior to neoadjuvant treatment and surgery, e.g. based on evidence such as physical examination, imaging, and/or biopsy.

This profile builds on Observation.

This profile was published on Fri Jul 19 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a active by The MITRE Corporation.
