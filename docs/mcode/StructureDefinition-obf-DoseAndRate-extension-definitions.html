9. doseamount
SliceNamedoseamount
Definition

The amount of medication taken per dose, as a quantity or range. Relative dose (e.g., per kg body weight or surface area) are indicated by specifying appropriate units (e.g., mg/kg or mg/m2 etc).

Control0..1

10. doserate
SliceNamedoserate
Definition

The speed with which the medication was or will be introduced into the patient. Typically the rate is used to describe an infusion, e.g,. 100 ml per 1 hour or 100 ml/hr.
