About the iCAREData™ Project

The overall goal of the ICAREdata™ project is to enable clinical oncology research by prospectively gathering high quality real-world data.

The ICAREdata™ project is a collaboration between MITRE, the Alliance for Clinical Trials in Oncology, and the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. The project is conducting a study in association with a randomized controlled trial (RCT), which aims to demonstrate the ability to calculate equivalent clinical trial endpoints using computable clinical treatment data.

As part of the study, an open source infrastructure and data-capture methodology are being developed and piloted to capture higher quality EHR-based treatment data, and permit the assessment of 1:1 correlation between RCT and real-world data.

iCAREData™ and mCODE™

This implementation guide intends to cover content necessary for iCAREData™ that is explicitly not part of the mCODE™ specification. Implementers should refer directly to mCODE™ for the specification of all mCODE™ elements.
