CancerDiseaseStatus

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/oncocore-CancerDiseaseStatus

The status of the cancer, in terms of whether it is stable, worsening (progressing), improving (responding), or in remission.

This profile builds on Observation.

This profile was published on Wed Mar 27 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
